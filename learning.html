Learning & Experience

As a culinary student, I’m constantly expanding my skills and gaining hands-on experience in the kitchen. Here’s a look at some of the training and experiences that have shaped my journey so far:

Education

Currently enrolled in the Culinary Arts Program at Jaminson University. My curriculum covers everything from knife skills and kitchen safety to advanced techniques in flavor pairing and plating.

Workshops and Certifications

  • Knife Skills Workshop: Mastered the basics of knife handling, sharpening, and different cutting techniques.
  • Plating and Presentation Techniques: Learned about the importance of color, texture, and balance in presenting dishes.
  • Food Safety Certification: Gained certification in food safety and sanitation practices.

Internship

Interned at Lumière Table, where I assisted in prep work, learned how to work on the line, and gained insights into the fast-paced environment of a professional kitchen.

Each experience has helped me understand the dedication and precision needed to create culinary masterpieces. I’m excited to continue building on these skills as I progress in my career!
